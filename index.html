Game Instructions

Player 1 Controls

Move: ← → ↑ ↓

Punch: Z

Dash: Double-tap ← or →

Toggle Flying: Double-tap ↑

Charge Ki: C

Charge Ki Attacks: X

Select Menu: Enter

Player 2 Controls

Move: A (Left), D (Right), W (Up/Jump), S (Down)

Punch: Z and M

Dash: Double-tap A or D

Toggle Flying: Double-tap W

Pause/Resume Game: Space
