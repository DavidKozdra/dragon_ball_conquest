Game Instructions

Player 1 Controls: A (Left), D (Right), W (Jump/Up), S (Down), Z (Punch), Double-tap A/D (Dash), Double-tap W (Toggle Flying)

Player 2 Controls: Arrow Keys (Move), N (Jump/Up), M (Punch), Double-tap Arrow Keys (Dash), Double-tap Up Arrow (Toggle Flying)

Press Space to Pause/Resume the game.
